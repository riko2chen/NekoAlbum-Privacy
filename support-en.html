Neko Album Support

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Neko Album is an on-device photo cleanup and compression app designed to help you sort photos more efficiently, compress images locally, and free up storage space without uploading your photo library for cloud processing.

1. Contact

If you need help, want to report a problem, or would like to share feedback, please contact: symeonchen@gmail.com

2. Support Notes

  • If a purchase does not appear immediately, please try the Restore Purchases option inside the app.
  • If photo access is limited, go to system settings and allow access to All Photos for the full cleanup experience.
  • If a legal page does not load, please check your network connection and try again.
